<body> Body The content inside the opening and closing body tags can be displayed to the screen.
<article> Article

<article> can hold content such as articles, blogs, comments, magazines, etc.

An <article> tag would help someone using a screen reader understand where the article content (that might contain a combination of text, images, audio, etc.) begins and ends.
<section> Section <section> defines elements in a document, such as chapters, headings, or any other area of the document with the same theme.
<nav> Navigation

The information within the <aside> enhances the information in <article> but is not required in order to understand it.
